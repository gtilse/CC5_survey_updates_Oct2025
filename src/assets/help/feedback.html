Feedback
Start the conversation: Here is all your feedback, starting with the most recent. Please don’t just read this feedback but use it as an opportunity for a follow-up conversation with your client. Rest assured that the more conversations you can have with clients after surveys to learn more and connect the better your survey results will be over time.

Search: You can search all your feedback history by client name or comment, e.g. search by “fees” or “good value” or “expensive” or any theme or comment.

Filter: press the blue filter button to start the search.

Client name: to see more, click on the client name. In the pop-up select the heading “SURVEYS” to see the full survey / experience history for that client (and any comments made by you or other staff following-up with the client). Select the heading “FOLLOW-UP” to contact the client. Click in the grey square, type a few comments from the conversation and select SAVE.

Call now: Select this phone icon for another route to the client card pop-up.

Flag: You can flag a comment to send a copy to the ‘Follow-up’ section of the app. You can unflag also if you change your mind.

Note: You can leave a note for a colleague that also has responsibility for that client (eg please follow-up and lets discuss). Please

We also rank the top three areas less satisfied clients (neutrals and detractors) would like improved to make them more likely to recommend you?
